MoveSH | Leveraging WeChat to Build a Modern Fitness Platform
WeChat Mini Program, Digital Strategy, Website
MoveSH is a digital platform where users can discover, search, book, and share over 1000+ fitness activities from 100+ venues in Shanghai.

The WeChat Mini Program (WXMP) was a game changer for MoveSH. With new functioanlity like one-time booking, dynamic pricing, and social sharing, the platform saw a 300% increase in users over a six month period.

Contact us to learn more about this project and how we helped MoveSH reach 10,000 users.
Concord Music | Upgrading for the Chinese Market
Website Design, Development, Hosting
Concord Music had been struggling with an outdated and VPN-only website for years. As one of their main marketing tools, it was woefully unrepresentative of their organization.

Our team worked alongside Concord to completely re-design, re-develop, and re-publish their website.

The new website is brighter, bilingual, has a modern feel, and is hosted domestically in China. These changes have increased the shareability of the site and attractiveness of the school to potential parents and students.
